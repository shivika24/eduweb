Who are we

Who are we

Our primary aim shall always be to develop the understanding of the subject by giving tools to memorize and other means to absorb fully the basics of a subject.

EduWeb is formed with the primary object to develop the requisite conceptual understanding of the subjects from practical or industry perspectives. A common problem being faced by the industry leaders, being small companies to large multinationals, is that most work force in accounting and related jobs like statutory compliance of various laws at local level and management skill set of routine tasks is not world class.

They lack expertise and requisite skill, necessary to operate in the practical world. This has led to a realization that primary focus of our tutors and coaching institutes has been to secure ranks with least botheration about the fundamental clarity. Our other courses are designed for students of professional courses such as Chartered Accountant (CA), Company Secretary(CS), Certified Financial Accountant (CFA), Certified Professional Accountant (Canada) and other global courses. Our goal is to create professionals with in-depth understanding of subject matter. .

The teaching role of faculty members reflects their centrality in addressing the primary educational mission among colleges and universities. Other institutional personnel increasingly have positioned themselves as educators to complement or enhance the traditional teaching role of faculty members.
